⭐ Core Features (Best for College Project)
1) Smart Doubt Solver

  Student types a question:

  AI explains the answer in simple language

  Gives examples

  Step-by-step solutions

  Example:

  “Explain polymerization in simple words.”

2) Notes Summarizer

  Student uploads or pastes notes, and AI:

  Summarizes long notes

  Highlights important points

  Makes short revision notes

3) Automatic Quiz Generator

  System:

  Generates quiz questions from notes

  MCQ, true/false, short answer

  Gives score & feedback

  Great for exam preparation.

4) Study Planner

  AI creates a study plan based on:

  Exam date

  Subjects

  Available study time

  Example:

  “Study 2 hours daily for 20 days.”

5) Revision Reminder System

  Reminds students:

  What to revise

  When to revise

  Pending chapters
